📊 Indian Mutual Fund Portfolio Dashboard

An institutional-grade, interactive HTML dashboard for analyzing Indian mutual fund portfolios. Upload your CAS, MF Central, or AMC statements to get fund-wise performance, allocation breakdown, action recommendations, tax opportunities, and a portfolio score out of 10.

About This Project

Welcome to my Indian Mutual Fund Portfolio Dashboard, a project developed to perform structured and comprehensive analysis of Indian mutual fund investments through CAS, MF Central, and AMC statement parsing.


The dashboard integrates fund-wise performance analysis (XIRR, returns, benchmark/category comparison), allocation breakdown (asset class, category, cap size), action recommendations, tax optimization opportunities, source verification, and a 3-tier scoring framework into an interactive analytical framework.


The objective of this project is to transform mutual fund statement data into meaningful insights and establish a standardized approach for evaluating mutual fund portfolios across categories, AMCs, and investment goals.


Through this initiative, I aim to demonstrate the application of financial analysis, mutual fund expertise, data visualization, and analytical problem-solving in the field of mutual fund portfolio analysis and investment planning.

From Data to Decisions

Mutual fund platforms and portfolio screeners excel at providing data. However, investment decisions require much more than numbers alone. Investors must understand fund quality, manager execution, overlap risks, expense efficiency, tax impact, and future growth prospects before building conviction.

This dashboard is designed as a research layer on top of mutual fund platforms. Rather than replacing portfolio screeners, it helps investors transform information into structured investment insights.

  • Move beyond NAV and understand the fund behind the numbers
  • Reduce hours of manual research and information gathering
  • Quickly identify underperforming funds and major risks

  • Assess whether expense ratio is justified by performance

  • Consolidate multiple research dimensions into one dashboard


🔍 Recommended Workflow

The most effective approach is to use this dashboard alongside mutual fund platforms and portfolio screeners.

  1. Use mutual fund platforms to discover and shortlist potential fund opportunities.
  2. Upload your CAS, MF Central, or AMC statement to the dashboard.
  3. Understand fund quality, overlap risks, expense efficiency, tax impact, and portfolio thesis.
  4. Review action recommendations and tax optimization opportunities.
  5. Use the consolidated insights and portfolio score to support investment decisions.

Key Philosophy

Mutual fund statements tell the story of the past. Investment decisions depend on understanding the future. This dashboard attempts to bridge that gap by combining fund analysis, performance review, benchmark comparison, risk evaluation, tax reasoning, and portfolio optimization into a single research framework.
